Evolution by Artificial Selection

How to Play:

  • A new game will generate three random Biomorphs
  • Make the Biomorphs evolve by selecting one of the Child Biomorphs
  • This will make it the Parent Biomorph and three more Children will be generated based on slight mutations of the Parent's "genome"
  • When selecting the Child Biomorphs, try focusing on one feature, like polygon radius or arm length, to illustrate the principal of evolution!

Our Biomorphs game was inspired by the game of the same name that was created for Richard Dawkin's 'The Blind Watchmaker' as a way to illustrate how slight mutations over many generations result in the evolution of different species.
